World-Class Facilities

Supporting excellence in education through state-of-the-art infrastructure

Our Campus Facilities

Spread across 10 acres of lush green environment, Greenfield International School boasts world-class infrastructure designed to provide students with the best possible learning environment. Our facilities are continuously upgraded to meet global educational standards while ensuring safety, accessibility, and sustainability.

Smart Classrooms

Our technology-enabled classrooms are designed to make learning interactive and engaging:

  • 80+ air-conditioned, digitally equipped classrooms
  • Interactive smart boards with multimedia content
  • Ergonomic furniture for student comfort
  • Optimal natural lighting and ventilation
  • Wi-Fi enabled with secure internet access
  • Capacity: 30 students per classroom

The classroom design promotes collaborative learning with flexible seating arrangements and dedicated spaces for group work.

Science & Computer Labs

Our well-equipped laboratories provide hands-on learning experiences:

Physics Lab

  • Modern equipment for mechanics, optics, and electricity experiments
  • Dedicated areas for different physics disciplines
  • Safety-certified apparatus

Chemistry Lab

  • Fume hoods and safety stations
  • Comprehensive chemical inventory
  • Digital measuring instruments

Biology Lab

  • Microscopes with digital imaging
  • Specimen collection
  • Interactive anatomy models

Computer Labs

  • 3 labs with 100+ high-speed computers
  • Programming and robotics stations
  • 3D printing and AI learning tools

All labs are supervised by qualified instructors and maintained with strict safety protocols.

Sports Facilities

We believe in the holistic development of students through sports and physical education:

Outdoor Facilities

  • Olympic-size swimming pool with trained coaches
  • 400m synthetic track for athletics
  • Football field with FIFA-standard turf
  • Cricket pitch with practice nets
  • Basketball and volleyball courts
  • Tennis and badminton courts

Indoor Facilities

  • Air-conditioned gymnasium
  • Table tennis arena
  • Yoga and meditation hall
  • Martial arts dojo
  • Squash courts
  • Chess and carrom room

Our sports program includes regular coaching, inter-school competitions, and professional training for talented athletes.

Learning Resource Center

Our state-of-the-art library is a hub of knowledge and research:

  • 25,000+ print resources including books, journals, and magazines
  • Digital library with 10,000+ e-books and online databases
  • Reading areas for different age groups
  • Computer stations for research
  • Audio-visual learning materials
  • Quiet study zones and group discussion rooms
  • Newspapers and periodicals from around the world

The library hosts regular book clubs, author visits, and reading promotion activities throughout the year.

Arts & Performance Facilities

We nurture creativity through exceptional arts infrastructure:

Visual Arts Studio

  • Spacious, well-lit art rooms
  • Pottery wheels and kiln
  • Digital art workstations
  • Photography darkroom

Performing Arts

  • 600-seat auditorium with professional sound and lighting
  • Music rooms with various instruments
  • Dance studios with mirrored walls
  • Drama room with stage facilities

Students can explore various art forms under the guidance of expert instructors and visiting artists.

Other Campus Facilities

Additional amenities that enhance the student experience:

Medical Center

  • Fully equipped infirmary
  • Full-time nurse and visiting doctor
  • Annual health check-ups
  • First aid training for staff

Cafeteria

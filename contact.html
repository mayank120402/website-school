Visit Our Campus

We welcome prospective parents and students to visit our campus and experience the Greenfield difference firsthand. Campus tours are conducted on weekdays between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

To schedule a visit, please call the admission office or fill out the contact form above. Please bring a valid photo ID when visiting the campus.

What to Expect During Your Visit:

  • Guided tour of our facilities
  • Meeting with academic staff
  • Information about our curriculum
  • Discussion of admission process

Frequently Asked Questions

The school administrative office is open from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. The academic day for students runs from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM (Monday-Friday) and 8:00 AM to 12:30 PM on Saturdays.

Meetings with the principal are by appointment only. Please contact the principal's office at +91 9876543214 or email principal@greenfieldschool.edu to schedule an appointment.

Detailed information about our transportation services, including routes and fees, is available from the transport incharge at +91 9876543213 or transport@greenfieldschool.edu. You can also visit the transport office during school hours.

Please call the school office at +91 9876543211 or email the class teacher to report an absence. For extended absences, a written application should be submitted to the class teacher upon the student's return.

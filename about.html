About Our School

Learn about our history, mission, and educational philosophy

Our History

Founded in 1995 by Dr. Ramesh Kumar, Greenfield International School began as a small institution with just 120 students and 8 teachers. Driven by a vision to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with character development, the school has grown exponentially over the years.

Today, we have over 2,500 students and 150 faculty members across a sprawling 10-acre campus. Our alumni have gone on to excel in diverse fields including medicine, engineering, arts, sports, and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and ethical values, preparing students to become responsible global citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that develops well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society through academic excellence, innovation, and service.

Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethical behavior in all our actions.

Excellence

We strive for the highest quality in all aspects of teaching, learning, and administration.

Respect

We value diversity and treat all individuals with dignity and compassion.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and embrace new ideas and technologies.

Service

We are committed to serving our community and making a positive impact.

Global Perspective

We prepare students to thrive in an interconnected world with cultural awareness.

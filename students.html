Peer Mentoring

Senior students guide and support younger students through various programs:

  • Academic mentoring for difficult subjects
  • Orientation for new students
  • Anti-bullying ambassadors
  • Career guidance sessions

This program builds leadership skills in mentors while providing valuable support to mentees.

Professional counselors provide academic, career, and personal guidance through individual and group sessions.

Career Guidance

Comprehensive career counseling, aptitude tests, and university placement support for senior students.

Health Services

On-campus medical care, health education programs, and annual check-ups for all students.

Admission Process

Join our community of learners and achievers

How to Apply

We welcome applications from students who demonstrate academic potential, curiosity, and a willingness to contribute positively to our school community. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward.

1

Inquiry & School Visit

Begin by submitting an online inquiry form or contacting our admission office to schedule a campus tour. This helps you understand our facilities and educational approach.

2

Application Submission

Complete the application form with all required documents. Applications are accepted throughout the year but early submission is recommended due to limited seats.

3

Assessment & Interaction

Depending on the grade level, students may be assessed through written tests, interviews, or classroom observations to determine readiness.

4

Admission Decision

Our admission committee reviews all applications and notifies parents of the decision within 7-10 working days after assessment.

5

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment process by submitting required documents and paying the admission fee within the stipulated time.

Admission Requirements

Age Criteria

  • Nursery: 3+ years as of March 31, 2024
  • Kindergarten: 4+ years as of March 31, 2024
  • Class 1: 5+ years as of March 31, 2024
  • Subsequent classes: Age appropriate for grade level

Documents Required

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original + copy)
  • Previous 2 years' report cards (if applicable)
  • Transfer certificate (for Class 2 and above)
  • Passport-sized photographs (4)
  • Aadhaar card copy (student and parents)
  • Medical history form

Assessment Details

  • Nursery-KG: Informal interaction
  • Classes 1-2: Basic literacy/numeracy
  • Classes 3-5: English, Math, General Awareness
  • Classes 6-8: English, Math, Science
  • Classes 9-12: Subject-specific tests + interview

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Merit Scholarships

Up to 50% tuition fee waiver for students with outstanding academic records in previous school.

  • Minimum 90% in previous class
  • Valid for one academic year
  • Renewable based on performance

Sports Scholarships

Fee concessions for students who have represented at district/state/national level sports.

  • 20-40% fee waiver
  • Must maintain sports participation
  • Requires certification from sports authority

Financial Assistance

Need-based aid for economically disadvantaged students with strong academic potential.

  • Family income verification required
  • Up to 75% fee concession
  • Community service component

To apply for scholarships, please submit the scholarship application form along with supporting documents by January 15, 2024. Decisions will be communicated by February 15, 2024.

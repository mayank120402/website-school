December 15, 2026 Event

Annual Sports Day 2023

The much-awaited Annual Sports Day was held with great enthusiasm on December 15th. Over 500 students participated in various track and field events, showcasing their athletic prowess. The highlight of the event was the march past by all four houses, followed by exciting competitions in sprint, relay, long jump, and other events.

Agni House emerged as the overall champion, closely followed by Neer House. Chief Guest Mr. Ravi Kumar, Olympic medalist, inspired students with his motivational speech about sportsmanship and perseverance.

November 28, 2025 Achievement

Greenfield Students Shine at Regional Science Fair

Our students made us proud by winning three awards at the Regional Science Fair organized by the Science Foundation. The team of Rahul Sharma and Priya Patel (Class 10) won the first prize for their innovative "Solar-Powered Water Purification System".

Other winning projects included "Smart Traffic Management Using AI" by Arjun Mehta (Class 11) and "Eco-Friendly Bioplastics" by Ananya Gupta (Class 9). The school has been invited to represent the region at the National Science Fair to be held in February.

December 5, 2024 Announcement

Winter Break Announcement

Greenfield International School will remain closed for winter break from December 25, 2023 to January 2, 2024. School will reopen on January 3, 2024.

Students are encouraged to utilize this time for recreational reading and family activities. Holiday homework has been assigned to all classes, which should be submitted on the first day after reopening.

Wishing all our students and their families a joyful and safe winter break!

November 15, 2024 Event

Annual Literary Fest Concludes

The three-day Literary Fest saw enthusiastic participation from students across all grades. Events included debate competitions, poetry recitation, creative writing, and storytelling sessions.

Renowned author Ms. Shobha De graced the occasion as chief guest and conducted an interactive session with senior students. The fest concluded with a dramatic adaptation of a classic play performed by our drama club.

October 30, 2024 Achievement

Student Wins State Chess Championship

We are proud to announce that Rohan Malhotra (Class 8) has won the Under-14 State Chess Championship held in Mumbai. Rohan remained undefeated throughout the tournament, showcasing exceptional strategic skills.

This victory qualifies him for the National Championship to be held in January. The school chess team has been training rigorously under Grandmaster Vishal Singh, who joined as coach this academic year.

October 10, 2026 Announcement

New Smart Classrooms Inaugurated

As part of our continuous infrastructure upgrade, we have transformed 20 classrooms into smart classrooms equipped with interactive whiteboards, digital content, and high-speed internet connectivity.

The new facilities were inaugurated by Dr. Ramesh Kumar, Chairman of Greenfield Group. These technology-enabled classrooms will enhance the teaching-learning experience through multimedia content and interactive pedagogies.
